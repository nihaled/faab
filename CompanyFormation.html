Company Formation

At Faab Partners, we provide end-to-end Company Formation services to help businesses establish a legal presence efficiently and compliantly. Our team guides clients through selecting the right business structure, obtaining necessary licenses, completing registrations, and ensuring adherence to all regulatory requirements.

We also offer ongoing support for documentation, approvals, and compliance updates, helping businesses operate smoothly from day one. By managing the complexities of company formation, we enable our clients to focus on growth, strategy, and achieving their business goals with confidence.

Mainland Company Setup

At Faab Partners, we provide end-to-end solutions for Mainland Company Setup to help entrepreneurs and businesses establish a legal presence in the UAE mainland. Our team guides clients through every step of the process, from business licensing and registration to compliance with local regulations, ensuring a smooth and efficient setup. We assist with selecting the right business activity, obtaining approvals, and managing documentation, enabling businesses to operate freely across the UAE market.

  • Business Licensing & Registration: Assisting with trade license applications, approvals, and legal documentation required for mainland operations.
  • Compliance & Advisory: Guiding businesses on local regulations, office requirements, and ongoing legal compliance to ensure smooth operation.
Freezone Company Setup (IFZA, SHAMS, RAKEZ, DMCC)

At Faab Partners, we specialize in Freezone Company Setup across major UAE freezones including IFZA, SHAMS, RAKEZ, and DMCC. Our team helps entrepreneurs and businesses establish their company efficiently, offering end-to-end support from license selection to registration and compliance. We ensure a smooth setup process, guiding clients through documentation, approvals, and legal requirements, allowing businesses to operate in a tax-friendly environment with 100% ownership and simplified procedures.

  • License & Registration Assistance: Supporting clients with business license applications, approvals, and registration in their chosen freezone.
  • Compliance & Advisory Services: Providing guidance on freezone regulations, corporate governance, and ongoing compliance to ensure seamless operations.
Offshore Company Setup

At Faab Partners, we provide expert services for Offshore Company Setup, enabling businesses to establish a legal entity outside the UAE while enjoying tax efficiency, asset protection, and confidentiality. Our team guides clients through the entire process, including jurisdiction selection, company registration, and compliance with international regulations. We ensure a smooth setup, helping businesses benefit from simplified operations, legal recognition, and global business opportunities.

  • Company Registration & Formation: Assisting with offshore company incorporation, documentation, and legal approvals in the chosen jurisdiction.
  • Compliance & Advisory: Providing guidance on regulatory requirements, annual filings, and best practices for asset protection and tax efficiency.
